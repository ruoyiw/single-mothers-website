What is self advocacy?
Self-advocacy is advocacy on one’s own behalf or in the case of a single mum, on behalf of her child or family. It is often individual – ‘I am speaking up on my own behalf’ – but it can involve group advocacy – ‘We are speaking up on our own behalf’.
Jimmy was such a shy kid. Just like me. I had a real battle at the school over getting extra support for him. It took over a year and I was so scared at first. But I got pretty good at it and Jimmy must have been taking notice, because he got elected class rep on the SRC and he loved it and was really good at it! Not so shy now.
